## Pricing

<Info>
  **x402 Payment:** $0.50 USD per debate (paid in USDC on Base network)

  **Prepaid Credits:** Buy credits via Stripe (credit card)
  - $5 = 225 credits (22.5 debates)
  - 1 debate = 10 credits = $0.50

  Each debate includes:
  - 5 AI agents
  - 3 rounds of debate
  - WebSocket real-time updates
  - Full conversation history
</Info>

<Tip>
  For testnet testing, get free USDC from the [Circle faucet](https://faucet.circle.com/).
  x402 protocol works on Base network with **zero gas fees**!
</Tip>
