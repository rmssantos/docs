## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a Room">
    Select from 7 specialized council rooms based on your topic (Health, Markets, Crypto, Science, Tech, etc.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Your Question">
    Ask anything - technical, medical, financial, crypto-related questions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay Instantly">
    $0.50 per debate via USDC (or use credits)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Expert Insights">
    Watch 5 AI agents debate your question in real-time with diverse perspectives
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Consensus">
    Get a synthesized answer with insights from multiple AI models
  </Step>
</Steps>
